2. About Task Booster

Task Booster is a casual Android app that offers simple brain games and daily tasks such as emoji counting, color memory and sequence tap.

Players earn an in-app virtual currency called Chillar by completing rounds and can spend it to unlock extra rounds and in-app boosts.

Important: Chillar is a virtual in-app currency only. It cannot be exchanged for real money, cannot be withdrawn, and has no real-world monetary value.

3. Virtual Currency (Chillar)

Chillar is used only inside the Task Booster app for:

  • Unlocking additional gameplay rounds.
  • Getting in-app boosts or extra chances in games.

Chillar cannot be:

  • Converted into cash, gift cards or any real-world items.
  • Transferred or sold to other users.
  • Withdrawn to bank accounts, UPI, wallets or any payment method.
